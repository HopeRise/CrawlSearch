The Door is a two-part celebrity series broadcast on the ITV Network in the United Kingdom, hosted by Amanda Holden (Wild at Heart) and Chris Tarrant (Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?). Keith Duffy and Dean Gaffney made it to the final with Duffy winning the show with Gaffney as the runner-up. Overview[edit] Six celebrities pass through a series of doors and take on a number of tough challenges. The last star standing claims a cash prize for their chosen charity. The show has been compared to classic TV show The Crystal Maze.
